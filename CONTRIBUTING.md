# Contributing

Thrystr accepts contributions only if the contributor certifies that the
project may use and relicense the contribution under the license terms in
`LICENSE`.

Use a Developer Certificate of Origin sign-off on every commit:

```text
Signed-off-by: Your Name <you@example.com>
```

By adding that line, you certify that you wrote the contribution or have the
right to submit it, and that the contribution may be distributed under both the
non-commercial and commercial terms used by this project.
